Homemade Mac and Cheese

Full of cheese and very little
poision this dish will have you
coming back for more!

Ingredients

  • Mac (some)
  • Cheese (lots)
  • Poison (very little)

Order of Operations

  1. Add all of the Macs to a large bowl
  2. Slowly mix in lots of cheese
  3. Poison to taste

Great for:

  • Parties!
  • Celebrations!
  • Work Events!
